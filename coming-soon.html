The science

It's cognitive. It was never character.

ADHD research keeps confirming what you already knew from the inside: the struggle lives in the brain's executive functions — working memory, planning, task switching, getting restarted — not in effort, intelligence, or willpower. "Try harder" was never the answer. Tools built for how your brain actually works are.

Ollin is a support tool designed around executive function, not a medical device or treatment.
